About This Detector

{{ disclaimer }}

Scope

This demo detects spatial and blending artifacts common in classic face-swap pipelines. It is designed for educational and research demonstration, not as a universal deepfake detector.

Attack Class

  • Face-swap style manipulations
  • Blending boundary artifacts
  • Over-smoothing and compression artifacts
  • Color inconsistency around facial regions

Known Limitations

  • Accuracy drops on newer diffusion-based and high-quality generative models
  • Performance depends on face detection quality and lighting
  • Heuristic mode is used until a trained checkpoint is available

Ethical Use

Use this tool to study manipulated media awareness and content verification workflows. Do not use probabilistic scores as legal evidence without expert review.
